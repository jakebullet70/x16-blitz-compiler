

## BLITZ

## Commodore

which
makes
## BASIC

## Most

whatsoever
## (although

## BLITZ
adds
to
standard
## BASIC),

a  few
minutes!
## Compiled
## BASIC
programs
cannot

listed
or
altered
by
users
and
so
compiling
provides
an
element
of
protection
against
unauthorised
copying
and
alteration

## BLITZ
is
available
for
the
## Commodore
## 64,
## 128,
and
## PET
computers
## (2001/3000,4000
and
## 8000

## Compiled

## BLITZ
## 64

## (called

## Quickly

## Large
## BASIC

## BLITZ

## Commodore
## BASIC,

## (the
